What Is Amazon FBA?

  • Amazon FBA (Fulfillment by Amazon) is a logistics solution where sellers send inventory to Amazon fulfillment centers, and Amazon handles storage, order fulfillment, and customer service.
  • Businesses of all sizes use FBA to simplify operations and enhance customer experience with fast shipping, inventory management, and hassle-free returns.

Common use cases for Amazon FBA:

  • E-commerce brands scaling their fulfillment operations.
  • Private label and wholesale sellers.
  • Retail arbitrage and online arbitrage businesses.
  • Dropshipping businesses looking to leverage Amazon’s logistics.
